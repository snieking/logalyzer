Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2024

This Privacy Policy explains how Logalyzer ("we", "our", or "the Service") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our Discord bot.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide:

  • Discord messages that mention the bot (to process your questions)
  • WarcraftLogs URLs shared in channels where the bot is present

Information Automatically Collected:

  • Discord server ID and channel ID (for bot functionality)
  • Discord user ID (to respond to your queries)
  • Usage data (number of queries, types of questions asked)

Information We Do NOT Collect:

  • Private messages or DMs (unless you directly message the bot)
  • Messages that don't mention the bot
  • Personal information like email addresses or real names
  • Payment information

2. How We Use Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Process and respond to your log analysis questions
  • Improve the Service's accuracy and functionality
  • Monitor for abuse and enforce our Terms of Service
  • Generate anonymous usage statistics

3. Data Storage and Retention

We minimize data storage:

  • Query messages are processed in real-time and not permanently stored
  • WarcraftLogs data is fetched on-demand and not cached long-term
  • Anonymous usage statistics may be retained for service improvement

4. Third-Party Services

Logalyzer integrates with:

  • Discord: For bot functionality. Subject to Discord's Privacy Policy
  • WarcraftLogs: For log data retrieval. Subject to WarcraftLogs' Privacy Policy
  • AI Services: For natural language processing (queries are processed but not stored by the AI provider)

5. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information only:

  • With third-party services necessary for bot operation (as described above)
  • When required by law or to protect our legal rights
  • In anonymized, aggregated form for analytics purposes

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, no internet transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Remove the bot from your Discord server at any time
  • Request information about data we may have collected
  • Request deletion of any stored data associated with you

To exercise these rights, contact us through our GitHub repository.

8. Children's Privacy

Logalyzer is not intended for users under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we learn we have collected such information, we will delete it promptly.

9. International Users

If you are accessing the Service from outside the country where our servers are located, your information may be transferred across borders. By using the Service, you consent to this transfer.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date. Continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our GitHub repository.
