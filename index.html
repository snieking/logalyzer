Frequently Asked Questions

Which WoW versions are supported?

Logalyzer supports WoW Classic (Vanilla) from WarcraftLogs. More to come soon.

Is my data private?

We only read publicly accessible WarcraftLogs data. We don't store your logs or personal information.

How accurate are the answers?

Logalyzer pulls data directly from the WarcraftLogs API. The AI interprets your question and fetches the exact data you need.

Is there a usage limit?

There are reasonable rate limits to prevent abuse.
